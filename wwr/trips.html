Whitewater Rafting

Available Trips and Activities

More Information about the Trips:

Boat Racing

 Race starting at the start of Pagsanjan River up to the end. Rates start at 800 pesos per person. One boat can accomodate a maximum of 6 people.

Half Day Trip

 Half day tour in Pagsanjan River. This package includes a licensed boatman and also serves as a tour guide. Rates start at 5,000 pesos for 4 person and 500 pesos additional fee for extra person. Boat can accomodate a maximum of 8 people.

Full Day Trip

 Full day tour in Pagsanjan River and in the caves. This package includes a licensed boatman and also serves as a tour guide. Rates start at 10,000 pesos for 4 person and 1000 pesos additional fee for extra person. Boat can accomodate a maximum of 10 people.

Trekking

 Trek in the Pagsanjan Forest. Package include a tour guide for 1000 pesos for 4 people including the entrance fee.
